ENERGY 2025

Solar & Wind Energy Harvester

A small-scale energy rig pulling power from both solar and a homemade wind turbine — the turbine built from a cereal box — generating around 3.7V. Outputs feed a capacitor circuit that stores and smooths the generated DC. A scrappy, hands-on energy-harvesting experiment.
